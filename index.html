About Startup Exchange

An interactive networking experience inspired by the World Café model, designed to connect students with the startup and venture capital world. Through a series of rotating, small-group conversations, students will:

  • Learn firsthand about the realities of launching and growing startups
  • Explore career paths and job opportunities in the startup ecosystem
  • Gain feedback and insights that can shape their own entrepreneurial ideas

Each table hosts a unique theme—ranging from early-stage challenges to scaling strategies, investing, and startup careers—creating space for open dialogue, practical advice, and authentic connections between students, founders, and investors.
