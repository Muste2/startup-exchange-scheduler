About Startup Exchange

Instead of traditional career fair booths, sit down for 20 minutes and have conversations with startup founders and venture capitalists. Ask about startup culture, pitch your ideas, get insider career advice, or explore potential collaborations—all in a casual, conversational setting. Whether you’re a techie looking for your next project or a business mind searching for a co-founder, Startup Exchange is your chance to network, learn, and spark new ventures—one conversation at a time.

📢 Note: Each time slot can accommodate up to 5 students per company table
